Rules

The game of Rock, paper, and scissors is based on trust, skill and determination. Each player must:
  • Choose either rock, paper, or scissors.
  • The browser will also choose either rock, paper, or scissors.
  • If both you and the browser pick the same thing, you tie.
  • Rock beats scissors, paper beats rock, and scissors beats paper.
  • Click on the Stats button to check on how you match up.
